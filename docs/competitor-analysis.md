## Section 1: AI Search Rankings

When a Canadian buyer searches "Canadian office furniture for my business" in AI tools (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overview):

| Rank | Company | URL | Why they win |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Source Office Furniture** | sourceofficefurniture.ca | Service-first framing ("free consultations"), strong national presence, consultation landing pages |
| 2 | **The Office Shop** | theofficeshop.ca | Clean product categories, good reception section, structured product pages |
| 3 | **POI** | poi.ca | "Connected solutions" framing, strong project portfolio ("Our Work" page), architectural language |

**What this means for BBI:** All three lead with outcomes and services, not products. BBI's current site leads with products. The gap to close: build a "Free Consultation" or "Free Design Layout" landing page and an "Our Work" portfolio page.

**Reference pages to study:**
- poi.ca/our-work/ — B2B project portfolio (HIGHEST PRIORITY to replicate)
- sourceofficefurniture.ca/services/free-consultations — service CTA framing
- theofficeshop.ca/product_category/reception/ — reception category page structure
- store.poi.ca/collections/task-seating/products/leap — individual product detail page writing style

## Section 2: Direct Dealer Competitors (Ontario)

These are the companies a BBI prospect would consider before or instead of BBI:

| Competitor | Positioning | Price point | Strengths | BBI advantage |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **atWork Office Furniture** | Closest direct competitor; design service + products, Ontario-wide | Similar to BBI | Design service, showroom, broad catalog | BBI has Global Furniture Group depth; commercial-grade brand pedigree |
| **McGowans** | Ontario dealer, generalist | Similar to BBI | Established Ontario relationships | BBI has online ordering + free design layouts |
| **Benton Commercial Interiors** | Local Southern Ontario | Similar | Local trust | BBI covers more of Ontario; has Shopify ecommerce |
| **Staples Business Advantage / Grand & Toy** | Commodity, volume-focused | Lower | Brand recognition, fast delivery, easy PO | BBI is commercial-grade; better for fit-outs vs. office supply replenishment |
| **IKEA for Business** | Price-sensitive | Low | Price, self-serve | BBI installs; commercial-grade build; warranty |
| **Amazon Business / Wayfair Pro** | Online only, no install | Low-mid | Speed, selection, no friction | BBI installs; can do complete space design; Canadian supplier preferred by institutions |

**BBI's actual advantages (from order data and service offering):**
1. Canadian supplier — preferred by institutions, non-profits, and government procurement
2. Ontario stock + delivery — ships from Ontario for faster delivery
3. Free Design Layouts & Renderings — a service Amazon/IKEA cannot offer
4. Installation available — critical for large workstation orders
5. Commercial-grade catalog — Global Furniture Group's contract line (565+ SKUs)
6. PO / invoice friendly — essential for institutional buyers
7. 5 Ontario locations — visible local trust signal
