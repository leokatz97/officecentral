## Priority order for design work

1. **Home page** — first impression, sets the brand tone.
2. **Product page (PDP)** — highest-traffic template; every SEO win lands here.
3. **Collection page (PLP)** — second-highest traffic; category SERP landings.
4. **Marketing landing page** — unlocks all 8 Wave 1 pages in one design.
5. **Generic content page** — unlocks About, FAQ, Our Work, Canadian-Made.
6. **Cart + Blog + Search + 404** — polish after core is locked.
7. **Account pages** — last, or leave as default.
