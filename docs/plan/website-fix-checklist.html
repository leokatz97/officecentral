Rules

Page Interconnection Rules

Apply when building or editing any page
  1. Breadcrumbs on all sub-collection pages: Home > Shop Furniture > [Category] > [Sub-collection] — BreadcrumbList JSON-LD included.
  2. Category pages link back and across: breadcrumb to vertical + "See all Business Furniture →" + "Can't find it? Call 1-800-835-9565"
  3. Industry pages cross-link to Business Furniture: each industry page has "Shop [Sector] Furniture →" button linking to relevant sub-collections (see Phase 1 cross-link mapping above)
  4. Shop vertical cross-links to industry pages: /collections/business-furniture includes contextual module linking to /pages/industries
  5. Every sub-collection page has a phone CTA block — "Can't find exactly what you need? Call 1-800-835-9565" + "Request a Quote →"
  6. Homepage surfaces Business Furniture categories (9 tiles) + OECM trust bar + 5 industry thumbnails
  7. Brand pages link to relevant products: each /pages/brands-* includes "Shop [Brand] products →" + 3–4 featured products

AI Search

AI Search Readability Audit

Make BBI citable by ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Google AI Overviews
Technical layer — structured data + crawlability
IDTaskStatus
AI-1llms.txt fileCreate at store root listing key pages, their purpose, and BBI's entity summary. Format: plain-text markdown. Allows LLM crawlers to orient quickly. 30 min, pure upside.
AI-2robots.txt auditConfirm no Disallow rules blocking GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot, or Googlebot. Shopify default allows all; verify no custom overrides.
AI-3Product schema (JSON-LD)Verify Product structured data on every product page: name, description, image, offers (price, availability, currency), brand, sku. Missing schema = invisible to AI Overviews.
AI-4Organization schemaAdd Organization JSON-LD to homepage + About page: name, url, logo, telephone, address (Richmond Hill ON), areaServed (Ontario, Canada), sameAs (officecentral.com, brantbasics.com). 1 hour — makes BBI a named entity.
AI-5FAQ schema on key pagesAdd FAQPage JSON-LD to OECM page, Design Services page, and top 3 blog posts. Q&A format is the most-cited content type in AI Overviews.
AI-6BreadcrumbList JSON-LDConfirm breadcrumb schema on all collection + product pages (partially done in Phase 2 — verify on product pages too).
Content layer — AI-readable copy
IDTaskStatus
AI-7Entity clarity on homepageAnswer in plain text within the first 200 words: who BBI is, what they sell, who they serve, and where they operate. AI models extract entity facts from above-the-fold content.
AI-8OECM page copyWrite a clear, factual paragraph: "Brant Business Interiors is a verified OECM supplier partner. Ontario school boards, hospitals, libraries, universities, and municipalities can purchase through BBI without open tender under the OECM framework." Highest-value AI citation target. 30 min.
AI-9FAQ blocks on product category pagesAdd 3–5 Q&A blocks per category page (e.g. "What is the best ergonomic chair for a school board?" on /collections/seating). Write answers in 2–3 sentences citing specific products or specs.
AI-10Spec completeness on product pagesEnsure all Hero 100 have: dimensions, weight capacity, materials, warranty, lead time. Ties into Phase 1b PE-2.
AI-11"Best of" and comparison copyAt least one page or blog post per major category that directly answers "best [category] in Ontario/Canada" with named products + reasons. Highest-citation format for ChatGPT shopping queries.
