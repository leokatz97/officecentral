## Recommendations

Ordered by severity:

1. **BUG-FIX: Strip `oecm-eligible` from all 593 products, re-apply only to verified OECM products.** This is already tracked as BUG-FIX-2 in the launch tracker (DEBT-03). Hard prereq for AI-8. The `/collections/oecm-eligible` smart collection returns the full catalog until this is fixed. Severity: blocks launch.

2. **NEW BUG: Audit and likely strip `industry:business` from the catalog.** 548/593 products carry this tag, making the `industry:` tag prefix meaningless for segmentation. Decide with Steve: (a) strip the `industry:` system entirely if it serves no functional purpose, or (b) design the proper industry-tag vocabulary and do a structured re-application. Either way, the current state is a bug. Suggested tracker row: `BUG-FIX-5` or fold into COLLECTION-CLEANUP-1 (launch tracker Step 9). Severity: nuisance until a filtered industry page is built.

3. **FIX: Add `Safco` and `Humanscale` to the canonical brand map.** 4 products carry `brand:safco` and 2 carry `brand:humanscale` — both legitimate brands not in `docs/strategy/brand-canonical-map.csv`. Before these products get type/room tags in COLLECTION-CLEANUP-1, their brand attribution should be confirmed against BBI purchase records and added to the map. Severity: low but needs resolution before TAG-APPLY-1.

4. **FIX: Re-tag `brand:heartwood` product to `brand:heartwood-manufacturing-ltd`.** One product uses the wrong slug. The correct tag is on 17 other products. Quick one-product fix; can be batched into COLLECTION-CLEANUP-1. Severity: low.

5. **INFO: `brand:global-teknion` on 26 unenriched products is expected.** VENDOR-NORMALIZE-2 confirmed: 0 enriched products retain this tag. The 26 remaining are unenriched and will be split to `brand:global-furniture-group` or `brand:teknion` per product when PE Pass 3 batches 3/4/6 run. No action needed now.

6. **STRUCTURAL GAP: `type:` and `room:` tag coverage is low.** `type:` covers only 39.8% of active products — the other 60.2% are invisible to type-based smart collections (`all-chairs`, `all-desks`, etc.), which drive "View all" CTAs on category pages. `room:` covers only 19.1%. This isn't a mass-application bug (the opposite — under-application), but it limits how effective smart collection filtering can be. Track as part of COLLECTION-CLEANUP-1 (Step 9): a systematic tag-application pass for `type:` and `room:` across the catalog.

7. **COLLECTION CLEANUP: `room:break-room` tag does not exist in the catalog.** The `room-break-room` smart collection (`TAG EQUALS 'room:break-room'`) has 0 products because no product carries this tag. Either add `room:break-room` to the `room:` vocabulary and tag appropriate products during COLLECTION-CLEANUP-1, or archive the break-room smart collection. Fold into COLLECTION-CLEANUP-1.
