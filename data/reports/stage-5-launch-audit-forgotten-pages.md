## Policy pages (PIPEDA/CASL compliance — FIX before launch)

Per `site-architecture-2026-04-25.md`:
> "Content must be filled in via Admin → Settings → Policies. PIPEDA/CASL compliance required."

Shopify auto-generates the policy URLs when content is entered. These are currently empty. **FIX required before launch** — a B2B site without privacy policy and terms of service cannot launch.
