What we do

Furniture that holds up in a busy clinical environment.

Brant Business Interiors supplies commercial-grade furniture for medical offices, dental clinics, physiotherapy practices, and long-term care facilities across Ontario. Every piece is specified for high-traffic use — cleanable surfaces, durable frames, and ergonomic options for staff who spend a full day on their feet or at a desk.

As an OECM Supplier Partner (Agreement 2025-470), Ontario hospitals and long-term care facilities can purchase directly from Brant Business Interiors without an open tender or RFP process. Our team provides a free space layout before you commit — so the room works on paper before a single piece ships.
