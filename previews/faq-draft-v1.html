OECM Procurement

OECM (Ontario Education Collaborative Marketplace) is a group purchasing organization for Ontario's education and broader public sector. As an OECM Supplier Partner (Agreement 2025-470), Brant Business Interiors offers pre-negotiated pricing that eligible institutions can access without issuing an open tender — saving procurement time and satisfying purchasing compliance requirements.

Ontario school boards, colleges, universities, hospitals, municipalities, and other BPS (broader public sector) organizations. If your organization is an OECM member, you can order from us directly under Agreement 2025-470.

Contact us at 1-800-835-9565 or info@brantbusinessinteriors.com. Reference Agreement 2025-470 and provide your organization name. We'll confirm pricing, generate an OECM-compliant quote, and process your PO.

Yes — there is no minimum order requirement under the agreement. OECM pricing applies whether you're ordering a single chair or furnishing an entire building.

Returns & Warranty

Standard stocked items may be returned within 14 days of delivery in original, unassembled condition. Custom-configured, special-order, and assembled items are non-returnable. Contact us before returning anything — all returns require prior authorization.

Warranty terms vary by manufacturer and product line. Most commercial-grade furniture carries a 5–10 year limited warranty on structural components. Specific warranty documentation is available on request — ask when you place your order or call 1-800-835-9565.
