Pneumatic Single or Double Monitor Arm Stand

 

This new monitor stand features the same sleek look as our existing 100-MA2C, with a neutral black frame and blue accents. The 100-MA1C is perfect for freeing up space on your desk and works well with both flat and curved monitors.

 

FEATURES:
• Detachable VESA plate (Drilled for 75x75 and 100x100mm)  for easier monitor installation
• Holds up to 32” (single arm 35”) large monitors
• Organize monitors in any combination of landscape or portrait orientations
• Compatible with optional laptop holder

 Desk clamp and/or grommet mount
• Recommended for deep worksurfaces or corner applications (30” deep or greater)
• 90° monitor swivel angle
• 180° monitor rotation
• 40° monitor tilt angle
• 22 lbs. weight capacity (per arm)
• 10yr warranty
• Finger touch height adjustment
• Dual arm 32” max. monitor (35” single arm)
