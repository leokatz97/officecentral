How this is computed

  • Scope: sales_owner ∈ {leo@venn.ca, leo@tryvault.com}.
  • Daily run-rate: revenue_cad ÷ billing_days_in_window, computed per customer.
  • Cliff: first_90_day_cutoff. After this date a customer contributes $0.
  • Actuals (April + May 1–10): proportionally allocated from revenue_cad across days in [first_revenue_date, latest_revenue_date] — totals tie exactly to your $43,423.76 booked.
  • Forecast (May 11 → Jun 30, plus Jul for visibility): daily rate × days remaining until each customer's cliff or end of bucket.
  • Caveat: Run-rate is optimistic — assumes every customer continues spending at their lifetime average until cliff. The "Going quiet" tab flags customers who've stopped transacting.
